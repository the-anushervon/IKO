About IKO 2025

The International Knowledge Olympiad (IKO) is a prestigious global competition that nurtures academic excellence and creativity. The National Round in Tajikistan, running from April 15 to June 30, 2025, invites students in Grades 3-11 to compete in Mathematics and English. This platform fosters critical thinking, collaboration, and innovation, offering a pathway to the global round and opportunities for scholarships, internships, and recognition.

Organized annually in online and offline formats, IKO ensures accessibility while upholding academic integrity, shaping future leaders and thinkers worldwide.

Competition Categories

IKO 2025 challenges students in two core subjects across five grade levels:

Mathematics

Grades 3-11 | From basic arithmetic to algebra and geometry, test your problem-solving skills.

English Language

Grades 3-11 | Excel in grammar, comprehension, and creative writing.


  • Category 1: Grades 3-4
  • Category 2: Grades 5-6
  • Category 3: Grades 7-8
  • Category 4: Grades 9-10
  • Category 5: Grade 11

Awards & Recognition

Excel and earn prestigious rewards based on your performance:

1st Place (85-100 points): International Medal & Certificate
2nd Place (75-84 points): International Medal & Certificate
3rd Place (65-74 points): International Medal & Certificate


All participants receive a Participation Certificate. Top performers may also unlock scholarships, internships, and a spot on the IKO Global Leaderboard.
